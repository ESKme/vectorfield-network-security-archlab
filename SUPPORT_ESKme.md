# Support the ESKme Projects

The work presented in this repository is published as open educational material.

The goal of the ESKme projects is to make cybersecurity concepts, security architecture thinking, 
and practical learning resources accessible to a broader community of learners, students, and professionals.

This repository is part of that effort.

---

## Why Support Matters

Projects such as this architecture study require significant time for:

- research and documentation
- architecture design and modeling
- technical validation
- writing and publishing open educational material

If you find this study or the broader **ESKme.net** projects valuable, 
you can help support the continued development of future materials.
